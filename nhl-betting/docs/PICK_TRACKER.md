## Sizing Rules

- **Method:** Quarter Kelly Criterion
- **Formula:** Size = 0.25 × [(b × p - q) / b] × bankroll
  - b = decimal profit per dollar wagered
  - p = historical hit rate
  - q = 1 - p
- **1 unit = $25**
- **Max single bet:** ~8.5u (Kelly naturally caps this)
- **Max daily deployment:** ~70% of bankroll
- **All bets are singles** — parlays are for fun only and not tracked

## Key Lessons

1. **Singles > Parlays** — Feb 25 parlay went 4/5 but paid $0. Same legs as singles = +$12.83.
2. **Hit rate filter is critical** — screen out high-assist players from UNDER bets, verify P/GP before any pick.
3. **Plus-money 1.5 plays are the highest-ROI strategy** — Nylander +235 and Necas +225 were the best bets on Night 1.
4. **Kelly sizes up on fat edges** — Malkin at +12.6% edge gets 8.5u, Bedard at +1.8% edge gets 0.7u. Let the math decide.
